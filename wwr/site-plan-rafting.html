Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote our White Water Rafting business to get more clients. We believe that through this website our clients will be able to be informed and gain interest in white water rafting. We predict that the increase of exposure from this website will increase our overall profit. Also on this website customers will find the times, and be informed on if we are open or not. They will also have access to see any specials that we may have.

Audience

The target customers for our business are college aged students or young adults who are seeking adrenaline boosting activities. They will most likely due to age not have a lot of money, so they will be selective in their decisions of the activities they do. We will be meeting their need for an activity that requires little to no effort on their part, but provides a thrilling environment. The targeted access to the site will most likely be on mobile devices. Our goal is to stand out as a worthwhile adventure for the college students to take with their friends. We guarantee that our customers will have a satisfactory experience that will be both fun and safe.
